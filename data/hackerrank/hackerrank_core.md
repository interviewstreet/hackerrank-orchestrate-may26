## HackerRank Core Policies
- **Account Deletion**: Users can delete their account in Settings > Delete Account. Google-linked accounts must set a password first via 'Forgot Password'.
- **Score Changes**: HackerRank support CANNOT manually change test scores or review answers. All grading is automated and final.
- **Adding Time**: To add time for a candidate: Tests > [Select Test] > Candidates tab > [Select Candidate] > More > Add Time Accommodation. Use multiples of 5%.
- **Rescheduling**: Candidates must contact the hiring company/recruiter directly to reschedule. HackerRank support does not have the authority to reschedule assessments for candidates.
- **Refunds**: Mock interview refunds are handled via help@hackerrank.com according to the refund policy.
